Open House Policy

Open Houses are permitted within the Canyon Pass community provided they comply with the following requirements listed below:

  1. Prior to scheduling an open house, the realtor must contact Yolanda Quiroz with Associa Arizona via email at Yolanda.quiroz@associaarizona.com and provide the following information with at least 3-5 business days in advance:
    1. The address of the home being shown in the open house.
    2. A map to the home being shown (base maps available from Associa Arizona or the gatehouse).
    3. Written directions from the gate house (go ___ miles on Dove Canyon Pass, turn left on ...)
    4. The date and times of the open house.
    5. Contact phone number of the realtor for the day of the event.
  2. Only one temporary, industry-standard open house sign is permitted at the property during an active showing. The sign may be displayed only while a realtor or their representative is on-site to show the property, and only between the hours of 8:00 a.m. and 6:00 p.m. on Saturdays and Sundays, except on the following major holidays:
    1. New Year's Day
    2. Easter Sunday
    3. Memorial Day
    4. Independence Day
    5. Labor Day
    6. Thanksgiving Day
    7. Christmas Day.
  3. No more than three open houses shall be scheduled for a day and large scale coordinated open house events are not permitted. This requirement is not applicable to a home tour for licensed realtors.
  4. Open house traffic should not impede the free flow of traffic within the community.
  5. No more than three "directional" signs are permitted per property to guide potential buyers to the property. These signs may be displayed only while a realtor or their representative is on-site to show the property, and only between the hours of 8:00 a.m. and 6:00 p.m. on Saturdays and Sundays.
  6. All open house signs must be placed on private property with the permission of the property owner. No open house signs are permitted on common property, including but not limited to, the community entrance, medians, and sidewalks.
  7. Open house signs must be removed immediately following the conclusion of the open house.
  8. All other marketing and advertising materials must comply with the community's governing documents and architectural guidelines.
